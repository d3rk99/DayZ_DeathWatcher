Joining Agreement

By joining Memento Mori you agree to play fair, respect other survivors, and accept that this is a hardcore environment. Death, loss, and setbacks are part of the story.

No cheats, no combat logging, no exploiting. Admin decisions are final. If something feels off, report it in Discord and we’ll dig into it.

How to Get In

1

Join the Discord

Hop into the Discord, read the rules channel, and grab the roles you need for the current season.

2

Install Mods & Connect

Use the mod list / Workshop collection posted in Discord, update your client, and connect via direct IP or server browser.
