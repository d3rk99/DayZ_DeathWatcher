New Survivor Guide

Read this first. These sections will be updated per season as rules and weather shift.

Section 01

Expectations

Slow survival pace, scarce meds, and weather that matters. Expect long walks and ambushes.

Section 02

Rules

  • No combat logging or ghosting.
  • No exploiting dupes or terrain glitches.
  • Respect restart timers; report bugs in Discord.

Section 03

Basics

Carry two heat sources, plan food routes, and keep a spare radio battery.

Section 04

Tips

  • Storms drift; watch cloud direction before crossing rivers.
  • Convoys drop unique loot — scout, don’t rush.
  • Travel light when weather warnings hit.
