About Us

Memento Mori was built by a small group of players who care more about mood, pacing, and stories than loot pinatas. We tweak each season by hand, from economy and weather to custom events and locations.

Expect long walks, bad weather, and the feeling that if you pull the trigger, it should mean something. If that sounds like your kind of misery, you’re in the right place.
